At Merchant Companies Bangladesh (MCB), it’s a Goal of this Company to ensure the overall Cargo Handling & Transportation Management to protect the interest of the client by removing any obstruction from on arrival of the consignment at any Inland River Port, Land Port & Sea Port of Bangladesh until completion.

We are vigilant guardians until shipment of exporter’s products and reaching the consignment to the final destination by using our chain management system through all Sea, River, and Land Ports of Bangladesh.

It’s been across more than 03 decades of experience, Hard Work, Honesty, Sincerity, Identity, Patronage, and foresight that has laid to this present branding position of this 3rd generation company.

MCB is one platform for monitoring 03 partner company namely Merchant Carriers & Traders, ARG Merchant Company Ltd & Merchant Distribution Company doing global standard work in Bangladesh.

Today’s MCB has become a pioneer and brand name in the fields of Bulk Fertilizer Bagging, Total Shipping, Power Generation – LPG-LNG- Heavylift equipment – Container Handling Contactory business, Central Road Transport Contractor of Ministry of Food, Govt. CPTU e-GP, BREB, BWDB Contractor & Exclusive Brand Distributor of LPG-LNG Product & Stockist of other product business in Bangladesh.

TeamMerchant of MCB has been achieved the satisfaction of our valued client by performing its work with the fullest efficiency, sincerity, honest, hardworking, and responsibility as per the work order. ‍So, any client can give us any assignment or mission and we assure the client that they will not face any hurdle and can have a perfect output without any worries.
